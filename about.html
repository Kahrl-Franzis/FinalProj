About Us

The owners of Naughty Nachos, Marianne Grace Borja, Jeanne Pearl Cainday, Pia Santiago, and Lourdes Panganiban, created the restaurant out of their passion for nachos. Their produced distinctive cheese sauce served as the impetus for opening their nacho eatery. Success stoked their appetite, and they quickly added more branches (and ideally still will).

Naughty Nachos' ambiance is energetic and appealing, complementing the menu's aggressiveness. Whether you're a spice lover or a fan of unusual taste combinations, Naughty Nachos invites you to enjoy the thrill of unexpected and delicious munching.

Naughty Nachos, an eating destination for people looking for something different, emphasizes the joy of flavor experimentation and the thrill of pushing culinary boundaries. So, if you're seeking for a unique nacho experience, Naughty Nachos guarantees a trip of taste and thrill.

Visit Us!

Since 2014, Naughty Nachos have begun slowly and steadily rising as it has now opened 3 branches for the people to visit. Although this map will only show one branch, that is located in the upper ground floor SMCF, Quirino Hwy, Novaliches, Quezon City, 1102 Metro Manila.

We are excited to welcome you to Naughty Nachos, where each visit promises a symphony of exquisite flavors, laughter-filled relationships, and the formation of nacho-infused memories.
